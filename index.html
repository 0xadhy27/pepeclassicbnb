How To Buy


1. Set Up Your Wallet

Setup your wallet using metamask or trustwallet to get started. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to metamask.io.

2. Get Some BNB

Have BSC in your wallet to switch to $PEPC. If you don’t have any BSC, you can buy directly on metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

3. Buy & Swap For $PEPC

Connect to pancakeswap. Go to Pancakeswap or poocoin in google chrome or on the browser inside your Metamask app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $PEPC token address into Pancakeswap or poocoin , select Pepe Classic, and confirm. When Metamask prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.

4. Enjoy

After the above step there is no need for more work, JUST HODL and ENJOY the ride to the moon.
